Comfort and safety from HIMO

The HIMO C26 electric bike will allow you to quickly and comfortably get to your place of work or study and not be tied to public transport. The bike has a solid construction and an impressive power reserve to become a reliable vehicle in modern urban conditions. The lightweight and durable frame, 26-inch wheels and a 7-speed transmission system allow you to confidently move not only on an asphalt roadbed, but also on a complex suburban highway.

Strong and lightweight frame

The HIMO C26 frame is made of durable and lightweight aluminum alloy and is characterized by high reliability, stability in emergency situations, excellent handling and comfortable movement on any road.

Combination of modes to increase the power reserve

The HIMO C26 electric bike has an increased power reserve. Its 10,000 mAh battery with an intelligent energy optimization system allows you to drive up to 60 km on a single charge in electric drive mode. When using physical force on some sections of the path, the range increases to 100 km. The maximum travel speed of the HIMO C26 is 25 km/h.

Reliable transmission

The 7-speed transmission system ensures smooth and precise gear shifting, convenient speed control and reliable operation. By shifting gears, you will be able to drive up the hill with minimal effort and slide down safely.

All information on the LCD display

All information on the LCD display. A large informative display placed on the steering rack displays all important information about the operation of the HIMO C26 in real time. You will be able to find out the current speed of movement, the charge level or the distance traveled. The display is protected from external factors according to the IPX7 standard and is not afraid of rain and dust.

Comfortable saddle

The HIMO C26 electric bike is equipped with a bicycle saddle from the famous brand Selle Royal. The three-dimensional gel structure of the saddle provides optimal elasticity and high comfort during riding.
